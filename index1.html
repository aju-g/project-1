About Us

Magic Momo serves authentic Himalayan-style momos using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes.

Momo is a type of steamed filled dumpling in Tibetan and Nepali cuisine that is also popular in neighbouring Bhutan, Bangladesh and India. The majority of Tibetan momo is half-moon in shape like jiaozi, while Nepali momo is normally round like baozi. Momo is usually served with a sauce known as achar influenced by the spices and herbs used within many South Asian cuisines. It can also be used in soup, as in jhol momo and mokthuk.
